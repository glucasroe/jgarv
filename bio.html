Biography

Countertenor Joshua Garvey has been praised for his supple, heart-breaking tone. Mr. Garvey has performed the roles of Amore and Valletto in Opera Vivente's production of The Coronation of Poppea. He reprised the role of Amore with Opera Vivente Academy in their Summer scenes program. During the same program, Mr. Garvey also performed in scenes as Tamerlano, Miles (The Turn of the Screw), Oberon (A Midsummer Night's Dream), and Ruggiero (Alcina). Mr. Garvey is currently working towards his Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance at Towson University, where he studies with nationally acclaimed tenor, Aaron Sheehan. With Towson University's Music for the Stage program, Mr. Garvey has performed in scenes from Rodelinda, L'inocornazione di Poppea, and Le nozze di Figaro. Mr. Garvey also participated in Towson University's international exchange with the University of Oldenburg, Germany, in a performance of The Three Penny Opera.
